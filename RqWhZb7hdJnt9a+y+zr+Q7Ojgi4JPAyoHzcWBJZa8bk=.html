PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 05, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

12345APP ("we," "us," or "our") is a privacy-focused service committed to protecting your personal data and online privacy. Our core mission is to provide secure, private network access. This Privacy Policy applies to our applications available on iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS.

App Store Compliance: This policy meets requirements for Apple App Store, Mac App Store, Google Play Store, and Microsoft Store.

Privacy URL: https://root-corporation.com/privacy

Legal Compliance: GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), COPPA (USA), PIPEDA (Canada), and other applicable international privacy laws.

BY USING OUR SERVICE, YOU CONSENT TO THE DATA PRACTICES DESCRIBED IN THIS POLICY.

2. DATA SAFETY SUMMARY (App Stores)

Privacy First: Our service is designed to protect your privacy. We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the service. All user data is stored exclusively in Australia and never leaves Australian jurisdiction.

Data Collected:
- Email address, account credentials (encrypted)
- Device information (OS type: Windows, iOS, Android, macOS; device model/version for compatibility)
- Payment transaction records (amount, date, status only - card details handled exclusively by Stripe)

Data NOT Collected (Privacy Protection):
- Browsing history or websites visited
- DNS queries or search history
- Traffic content or communications
- Bandwidth usage or data transfer amounts
- Cookies or tracking identifiers
- Payment card details or financial information (handled exclusively by Stripe)
- Location data (see Location Data Disclosure below)
- Personal files or contacts
- Photos, videos, or audio files

Location Data Disclosure: LOCATION data is accessed by the app but not disclosed in privacy policy. We do not collect or store your location. Location data is used solely on-device to create encrypted tunnels by selecting optimal servers based on your approximate region. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing: We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share your data with advertisers. Limited sharing only with payment processors (Stripe, Wise) and email service providers for essential account communications.

Security: Data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3, Ed25519).

Data Storage Location: All user data stored exclusively in Australia. Global encrypted tunnel servers do NOT store any user data.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information:
- Email address (required for account creation and communications)
- Password (encrypted using Ed25519 cryptographic signatures)

Payment Transaction Records:
- Transaction amount, currency, date, and status only
- Payment card details are forwarded to Stripe at frontend and are NOT stored on our servers
- We never collect, store, or have access to your payment card information
- Payout information for affiliate rewards (processed by Wise)

Usage Data:
- Account creation and access period expiration dates
- Purchased access package and status
- Device connection count and timestamps

Technical Data:

No-Logs Policy:

- We only retain minimal connection metadata necessary for service operation

ENCRYPTED TUNNEL DATA COLLECTION DISCLOSURE (App Store Compliance):

What user information is collected through the encrypted tunnel connection?
Our encrypted tunnel service is designed with privacy as the core principle. During tunnel operation:
- We do NOT collect, monitor, or log any websites you visit
- We do NOT collect, inspect, or store any of your internet traffic content
- We do NOT collect or log DNS queries or search history
- We do NOT track or record your original IP address after initial authentication
- We do NOT monitor bandwidth usage or data transfer amounts
- We do NOT use any tracking technologies, analytics, or advertising identifiers
The only data collected is minimal connection metadata (connection timestamp and duration) solely for service operation and troubleshooting.

For what purposes is this information collected?
The minimal connection metadata we collect serves these specific purposes only:
- Service Authentication: To verify your subscription status and authorize encrypted tunnel access
- Connection Management: To enforce device limits per account (maximum 5 concurrent devices)
- Service Quality: To identify and resolve technical issues affecting your connection
- Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized access or abuse of the service
We do NOT use any collected data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the encrypted tunnel service itself.

Is data shared with third parties? Where is it stored?
- Third-Party Sharing: We do NOT sell, rent, or share your encrypted tunnel usage data with any third parties. We do NOT share data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies. The only third parties involved are payment processors (Stripe for payments, Wise for affiliate payouts) who only receive payment transaction data, never tunnel usage data.
- Data Storage Location: All user account data is stored exclusively in Australia under Australian privacy law jurisdiction. Our encrypted tunnel servers located globally do NOT store any user data whatsoever - they only route encrypted traffic in real-time. No logs, no browsing history, no user information is retained on tunnel servers.
