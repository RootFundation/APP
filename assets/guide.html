Account Setup Guide

Create an international Apple ID, Google Account, or Microsoft Account to download apps from your preferred platform store.

Never sign out of your Apple ID from Settings — only switch accounts within the App Store. Your iCloud data will remain safe.

1

  1. You must use a brand new email address that has never been used with any Apple ID before. Create a fresh email (Outlook, Hotmail, Gmail, etc. — do not use email providers from restricted regions such as QQ Mail or 163 Mail).
  2. Prepare a phone number that can receive SMS verification codes — any phone number that has not been registered with an Apple ID will work, from any country/region.
  3. You will need an iPhone to complete the activation.
  4. You will need a valid Australian address and Australian phone number for later steps — visit the address generator at https://www.meiguodizhi.com/au-address to generate these. If the website is unavailable, search for "Australia address generator" on www.bing.com.

2

Create Apple ID in Browser

  1. Open a browser on any device in incognito/private mode and go to account.apple.com/au (Google Chrome or Edge is recommended for the best experience). Click "Create Your Apple Account" in the top right corner.
  2. Fill in your first name and last name (these can be anything).
  3. Select "Australia" as the country/region.
  4. Enter your date of birth (must be 18 years or older).
  5. Enter your new email address — the email must never have been used to register any Apple ID before. Make sure it can receive verification codes.
  6. Create a password (must be at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols).
  7. Enter your phone number — this will be used to receive the SMS verification code. It can be any number (it does not need to be an Australian phone number). This is different from the phone number you will enter later for the shipping address. Click "Continue".
  8. Verify both your email and phone number with the codes Apple sends you.

  2. After submitting, your Australian Apple ID registration on the web is complete.

Prepare Your iPhone

  2. Go to Settings > General > Language & Region — set Language to "English (Australia)" and Region to "Australia".
  3. Open the App Store, tap the profile icon in the top right, scroll down, and tap "Sign Out" to sign out of your current Apple ID in the App Store only (this does not affect your system Apple ID in Settings).

5

Sign In & Activate

  1. In the App Store, tap the profile icon in the top right and sign in with your newly registered Apple ID. Select any free app to download. A prompt will appear saying "This Apple ID has not yet been used in the iTunes Store".
  2. Tap "Review". Select "Australia" as the region (you must select the correct region), agree to the terms, and tap "Next". Do not select any payment method. Fill in the Australian address and phone number from Step 1 — the phone number here must be an Australian number from the generator, not your personal number.
  3. Tap "Next" to complete. Your Australian Apple ID is now fully activated and ready to use.

1

Preparation

You will need a working phone number for verification and a backup email address (optional). Make sure you have access to the Google Play Store on your Android device. Use a browser or the Gmail app to create your new account.

2

Create a Google Account

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "Australia". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "United States". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "Hong Kong". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

3

Add Account to Your Device

On your Android device, go to Settings > Accounts (or Users & accounts). Tap "Add account" and select Google. Sign in with your new Google account credentials. This will link the account to your device and the Play Store.

4

Switch Account in Play Store

Open the Google Play Store. Tap your profile icon in the top right and tap the down arrow next to your name. Select your new Google account from the list. The Play Store will reload with content available for that account's region.

5

Download Your App

Search for the app in the Play Store while signed in with your new account. Tap "Install" to download. If prompted, accept the terms of service. The app will be linked to your new Google account for future updates.

You can have multiple Google accounts on your Android device simultaneously. Switch between them in the Play Store without removing any account.

Preparation

2

Create a Microsoft Account

3

Sign In on Windows

On your Windows PC, open Settings > Accounts > "Email & accounts" and click "Add a Microsoft account". Sign in with your new credentials. Alternatively, you can sign in directly through the Microsoft Store app.

4

Open the Microsoft Store

Open the Microsoft Store from the taskbar or Start menu. Click your profile icon in the top right corner and make sure you are signed in with your new Microsoft account. If you have multiple accounts, select the correct one.

5

Download Your App

Search for the app in the Microsoft Store. Click "Get" or "Install" to download. If this is your first download, you may need to accept the terms of service. The app will be associated with your Microsoft account for future updates.

You can also download the app directly from the download section above without using the Microsoft Store. The direct download works without a Microsoft account.
