Account Setup Guide

Create an international Apple ID, Google Account, or Microsoft Account to download apps from your preferred platform store.

1

Preparation

Prepare an email address (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) that is not already linked to an Apple ID. You will also need a phone number that can receive SMS verification codes. Use a browser (Safari or Chrome) on your computer or phone.

2

Register Your Account

Visit account.apple.com in your browser and click "Create Your Apple ID". Select "Australia" as the country/region. Fill in your name, email, password, and phone number. Verify both your email and phone number with the codes Apple sends you.

Visit account.apple.com in your browser and click "Create Your Apple ID". Select "United States" as the country/region. Fill in your name, email, password, and phone number. Verify both your email and phone number with the codes Apple sends you.

Visit account.apple.com in your browser and click "Create Your Apple ID". Select "Hong Kong" as the country/region. Fill in your name, email, password, and phone number. Verify both your email and phone number with the codes Apple sends you.

3

Add a Shipping Address

After registration, go to "Payment & Shipping" in your account settings. Add an Australian shipping address. You can use any valid address, for example: 1 Infinite Loop, Sydney NSW 2000. Set payment method to "None".

After registration, go to "Payment & Shipping" in your account settings. Add a shipping address using a US tax-free state: Alaska (99801), Montana (59001), Oregon (97201), or Delaware (19901). You can use any valid street address in these states. Set payment method to "None".

After registration, go to "Payment & Shipping" in your account settings. Add a Hong Kong shipping address. You can use any valid address, for example: 1 Queen's Road, Central, Hong Kong. Set payment method to "None".

4

Sign In with New Apple ID

On your iPhone or iPad, open the App Store. Tap the profile icon in the top right, scroll down, and tap "Sign Out". Then sign in with your new Apple ID. Do NOT sign out from Settings — only sign out from the App Store.

5

Activate Your Account

Search for and download any free app from the Australian App Store. A "Review" prompt will appear — tap "Review" and agree to the terms. Enter your Australian address and postcode again. Once completed, your Australian Apple ID is fully activated and ready to use.

Search for and download any free app from the US App Store. A "Review" prompt will appear — tap "Review" and agree to the terms. Enter your US address and zip code again. Once completed, your US Apple ID is fully activated and ready to use.

Search for and download any free app from the Hong Kong App Store. A "Review" prompt will appear — tap "Review" and agree to the terms. Enter your Hong Kong address again. Once completed, your Hong Kong Apple ID is fully activated and ready to use.

Never sign out of your Apple ID from Settings — only switch accounts within the App Store. Your iCloud data will remain safe.

1

Preparation

You will need a working phone number for verification and a backup email address (optional). Make sure you have access to the Google Play Store on your Android device. Use a browser or the Gmail app to create your new account.

2

Create a Google Account

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "Australia". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "United States". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

Visit accounts.google.com/signup in your browser. Enter your first and last name, choose a Gmail username, and set a strong password. When prompted for your country, select "Hong Kong". Complete phone verification to finish creating your account.

3

Add Account to Your Device

On your Android device, go to Settings > Accounts (or Users & accounts). Tap "Add account" and select Google. Sign in with your new Google account credentials. This will link the account to your device and the Play Store.

4

Switch Account in Play Store

Open the Google Play Store. Tap your profile icon in the top right and tap the down arrow next to your name. Select your new Google account from the list. The Play Store will reload with content available for that account's region.

5

Download Your App

Search for the app in the Play Store while signed in with your new account. Tap "Install" to download. If prompted, accept the terms of service. The app will be linked to your new Google account for future updates.

You can have multiple Google accounts on your Android device simultaneously. Switch between them in the Play Store without removing any account.

Frequently Asked Questions

No. Android supports multiple Google accounts. You can add the new account alongside your existing one and switch between them in the Play Store.

No. Adding a new Google account does not affect your existing apps, contacts, photos, or any other data. Each account operates independently.

You can install the APK directly from the download section without a Google account. However, using the Play Store ensures automatic updates and verified installations.

1

Preparation

You will need a working email address or phone number. Make sure your Windows PC is connected to the internet. You can create a Microsoft account through a browser or directly from Windows Settings.

2

Create a Microsoft Account

Visit account.microsoft.com and click "Create a Microsoft account". Enter your email address or choose "Get a new email address" for an Outlook account. Set a strong password. When prompted for your country/region, select "Australia". Complete the verification steps.

Visit account.microsoft.com and click "Create a Microsoft account". Enter your email address or choose "Get a new email address" for an Outlook account. Set a strong password. When prompted for your country/region, select "United States". Complete the verification steps.

Visit account.microsoft.com and click "Create a Microsoft account". Enter your email address or choose "Get a new email address" for an Outlook account. Set a strong password. When prompted for your country/region, select "Hong Kong". Complete the verification steps.

3

Sign In on Windows

On your Windows PC, open Settings > Accounts > "Email & accounts" and click "Add a Microsoft account". Sign in with your new credentials. Alternatively, you can sign in directly through the Microsoft Store app.

4

Open the Microsoft Store

Open the Microsoft Store from the taskbar or Start menu. Click your profile icon in the top right corner and make sure you are signed in with your new Microsoft account. If you have multiple accounts, select the correct one.

5

Download Your App

Search for the app in the Microsoft Store. Click "Get" or "Install" to download. If this is your first download, you may need to accept the terms of service. The app will be associated with your Microsoft account for future updates.

You can also download the app directly from the download section above without using the Microsoft Store. The direct download works without a Microsoft account.

Frequently Asked Questions
